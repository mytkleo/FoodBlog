Reviews

Experiences and ratings for each convenience store.

7 11

A classic convenience store that all of us grew up with. Every item sold in this store are so iconic, especially the Slurpees and Gulps. Price range of the items are affordable and can be bought by a broke college student (Me).

Alfamart

Alfamart is the kind of convenience store that you come across when you are out travelling with your friends and family. For me, this is always one of our commong stopovers whenever we travel. My experience was good and I remember how kind and welcoming the staff were.

Uncle John's

Two words, Kariman and Chicken. Or was that three words? Anyways, Uncle John's, commonly known as Mini Stop before the name change, was one of my go to convenience stores whenever I stay at school because of projects or practices. Every meal sold is worth it and truly a 10/10 experience.

Lawson

Lawson convenience store is a popular chain known for its wide variety of snacks, drinks, and ready-to-eat meals, making it a convenient stop for a quick bite or everyday essentials. With its clean and organized stores, along with friendly and helpful staff, Lawson provides a reliable and satisfying shopping experience for customers on the go.

AllDay

AllDay is a popular retail chain offering a wide range of products such as snacks, drinks, and household essentials, with many branches open 24/7 for convenience. The stores are known for their affordable prices and friendly staff, making it a go-to place for quick and easy shopping needs.

FamilyMart

FamilyMart convenience store in the Philippines offers a wide range of products from snacks to groceries and is known for its clean and well-stocked stores. With numerous branches across the country, it provides a convenient shopping experience for customers on-the-go.
